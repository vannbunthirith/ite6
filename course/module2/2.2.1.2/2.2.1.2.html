ESD Tools

There are two ESD tools: the antistatic wrist strap and the antistatic mat. The antistatic wrist strap protects computer equipment when grounded to a computer chassis. The antistatic mat protects computer equipment by preventing static electricity from accumulating on the hardware or on the technician.

Figure 1 on this page shows an image of a technician wearing an antistatic wrist strap while working on a computer. An antistatic wrist strap is used to prevent ESD damage to computer equipment.Figure 2 on this page is an image of an antistatic mat. An antistatic mat is used to stand on or to place hardware on to prevent electricity from building up.
