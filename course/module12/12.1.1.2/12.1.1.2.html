Activity – Identify Malware Types

.

The figure on this page is an interactive activity that checks the learner’s understanding of malware types. Adware, usually distributed by downloading online software that displays most often in a pop-up window that is difficult to control. Spyware, used to gather information about a user and send the information to another entity, without the user’s consent. Rootkit, program used by hackers to gain Administrator-account level access to a computer. It is very difficult to detect. Ransomware, denies access to the infected computer system and requires payment for the restriction to be removed. Worm, this malware is a self-replicating program that spreads automatically. It slows or disrupts network operations.
