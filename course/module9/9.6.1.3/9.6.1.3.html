Test the Theory to Determine Cause

After you have developed some theories about what is wrong, test your theories to determine the cause of the problem. The figure shows a list of quick procedures that can determine the exact cause of the problem or even correct the problem. If a quick procedure does not correct the problem, research the problem further to establish the exact cause.

The figure on this page is a table that lists step 3 in troubleshooting laptop and mobile device problems, test the theory to determine cause. Common steps to determine cause for laptop problems: Use AC adapter with laptop, replace battery, reboot the laptop, check BIOS settings, disconnect and reconnect the cables, disconnect peripherals, toggle Num Lock key, remove and reinstall RAM, Caps Lock key is on, check for non-bootable media in a boot device. Common steps to determine cause for mobile device problems: Restart the mobile device, plug the mobile device into an AC outlet, replace the mobile device battery, remove any removable battery and reinstall it, clean the speaker, microphone, charging port, or other connection ports.
