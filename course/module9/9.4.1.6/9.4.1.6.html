Connection type: NFC, description: Mobile devices communicate with each other when they are touched together. Connection type: Tether, Description: this uses your cellular phone as a modem for another device, such as a tablet or laptop. The connection is made over a USB cable or Bluetooth. Connection type: Micro/mini USB, Description: devices charge and transfer data using this wired connection. Connection type: Bluetooth, Description: this wireless connection allows data exchange over a distance of up to 33 feet (10 m). Connection type: Lightning, description: This wired connection allows Apple devices to connect to host computers and other peripherals. Connection type: Infrared, description: This connection allows mobile devices to remotely control devices, such as TV or audio equipment.
