Cellular WAN

Laptops with integrated cellular WAN capabilities require no software installation and no additional antenna or accessories. When you turn on the laptop, the integrated WAN capabilities are ready to use. If the connection is not active, look for a switch on the front face or on the side of the laptop to enable the connection.

Many cell phones provide the ability to connect other devices, as shown in Figure 1. This connection, known as tethering, can be made using Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or by using an USB cable.
