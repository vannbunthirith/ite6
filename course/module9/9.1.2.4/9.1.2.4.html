Webcam and Microphone

Most laptops today have a webcam and microphone built in. The webcam is normally positioned at the top, center of the display, as shown in the figure. The internal microphone can often be found next to the webcam. Some manufactures may place the microphone next to the keyboard, or on the side of the laptop.

The figure on this page shows two images. The first image shows a person using a webcam on a laptop to communicate with another person in a different location. The second image shows a magnified view of the top of a laptop display highlighting the webcam and two microphones, one on either side of the webcam.
