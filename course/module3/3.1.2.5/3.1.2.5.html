Step 1, choose the proper motherboard standoffs for the case. Step 2, install the standoffs in the computer case at the same locations of the mounting holes in the motherboard. Step 3, install the I/O connector plate in the back of the computer case. Step 4, align the I/O connectors on the back of the motherboard with the openings in the I/O plate. Step 5, align the screw holes of the motherboard with the standoffs. Step 6, insert all the motherboard screws and hand-tighten all the screws before tightening with a screwdriver.
