Types of Adapter Cards

Adapter cards add functionality to a computer. There are many different types of adapter cards including video, Ethernet and wireless network, sound, TV tuner, video capture, external port such as USB, FireWire, Thunderbolt, and more.

Adapter cards are inserted into the following expansion slots on a motherboard:

  • PCIe - PCI Express has four types of slots; x1 (Figure 2), x4, x8, and x16 (Figure 3). Notice how these PCIe slots vary in length from shortest (X1) to longest (x16) respectively.
