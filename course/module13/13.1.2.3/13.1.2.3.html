Do: Explain why you have put the customer on hold, ask permission from the customer to put him or her on hold, give the customer some idea of how long you think it will be until you can come back, explain that you will have to put the customer on hold, and allow the customer to finish talking. Do Not: Put the customer on hold without telling the customer what you are about to do, talk while the customer is talking, begin answering a question before the customer has stopped talking, or put the customer on hold without asking permission from the customer.
