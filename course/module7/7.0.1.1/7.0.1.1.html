Chapter 7: Networking Concepts

This chapter provides an overview of network principles, standards, and purposes. To meet the expectations and needs of your customers and network users, you must be familiar with networking concepts. You will learn the basics of network design and how some components affect the flow of data on a network. This knowledge will help you successfully design, implement, and troubleshoot networks.
